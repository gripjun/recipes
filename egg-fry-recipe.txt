Ingredients:
	1. one or two egg
	2. olive oil
	3. some salt


How to cook it:
	1. you put your frying pan on the stove and get it hot for a five minutes.
	2. pour olive oil into pan about one or two spoon.
	3. break egg and put contents of egg into pan and boil it.
	4. you can choose how much it fried.
	5. finish it with salt on the surface of fried egg.
